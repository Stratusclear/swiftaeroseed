About Swift Aeroseed

We are an agricultural drone seeding company that specializes in cover crops and ecological services.

We improve soil health by overseeding into maturing cash crops (wheat, soy, corn) with a drone using multispecies cover crops. Cover crops are important for soil health because they keep living roots in the soil, preventing erosion and encouraging microbial growth. Microbes create spongy soils that soak up rain and improve water infiltration, and crop production, saving farmers money on inputs and increasing their profits with higher yields.
